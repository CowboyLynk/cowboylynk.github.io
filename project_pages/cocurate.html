Co-Curate

A thoughtfully-designed recipe app.

About

A living recipe book curated by you and your friends. Stop reading reviews and ratings, cook what your friends are cooking and start a conversation about it.

Key features:

  • See what your friends are cookin'
  • AI assistant for ingredient substitutions (and more)
  • View ingredient amounts directly alongside each step
  • Swipe through ingredients for easy step navigation
  • In-app timers
  • Add recipies by URL (skip the ads and paywalls)
