Co-Curate

A thoughtfully-designed recipe app.

About

A living recipe book curated by you and your friends. Forget reviews and ratings; cook things your friends have made and spark conversations over shared meals.

Key features:

  • See What Friends Are Making: Keep up with your friends' latest kitchen creations.
  • AI Ingredient Swaps: Run out of something? Let AI handle it.
  • Easy step Navigation: Swipe through steps while you cook.
  • Steps with Ingredients: No more back-and-forth—see ingredients alongside each step.
  • Built-In Timers: Timers that keep you on track without leaving the app.
  • Quick Recipe Import: Add recipes by URL—skip the ads, cook right away.
