About

Over the course of my four years at MIT, I've had the honor of working on this annual pi day tradition to announce the MIT decision release date for prospective students.

My responsibilities included the cinematography, 2D/3D visual effects, and editing of the annual MIT Admissions video.

Altogether, the videos have reached over 394,000 views on YouTube and over 6.6 million views on online articles.
