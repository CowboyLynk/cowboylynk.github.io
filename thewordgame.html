About

The Word Game is a word puzzle game. The goal is to change the given word into a new word through one of four different moves. To play, you can...

1. Add a letter: PLANT -> PLANET
2. Subtract a letter: GONE -> ONE
3. Swap a letter: PEAR -> BEAR
4. Rearrange the letters: DARE -> READ

However, there are some restrictions. You may not...
1. Simply pluralize of change tense: GRIT -> GRITS | LOVE -> LOVED
2. Play a word that has already been played
3. Play a word that would be a valid play on the last word (Double Play): RILE -> FILE -> MILE
